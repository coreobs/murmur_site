Requesting a Mumble

A template for requesting Mumble Servers through our Discord.

The Template

We don't have any restrictions on the use of the Free Mumble Servers, we just ask that it's loosely Team Fortress 2 related. If you are unsure on what to put, you can use the Template below:

Username: 
Desired Mumble Name: 
Custom Password (not required):
What do you want to use the server for?:

Submitting

We use our Discord's Tickety Bot to take Mumble requests, Support Requests and more. Staff will respond as soon as possible, but the setup can be a day or more depending on our current workload.

Don't have our Discord or don't want to join it?

Respawn League Discord

If you don't wish to join our Discord, you can put up a post on our community forum. Please note that you will have to make an account to post for obvious reasons.
